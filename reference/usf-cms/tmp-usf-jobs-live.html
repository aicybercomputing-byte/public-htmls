Why the AI era boosts — not replaces — computing careers

BLS finding

Productivity creates demand

AI-assisted productivity lowers software costs, expanding the market and boosting demand for the very developers who build AI tools — a virtuous cycle for computing graduates.
CompTIA 2025

Data & AI: fastest-growing segment

Data science, ML, and database roles are Florida’s fastest-growing tech category at 5.6% annually — 3.6× the national benchmark. Directly aligned with Bellini programs.
Sources: BLS Monthly Labor Review Feb 2025 (Machovec, Rieley & Rolen) - national projections 2023-2033. CompTIA State of the Tech Workforce 2025 (Lightcast / BLS) - Florida state-level figures.
