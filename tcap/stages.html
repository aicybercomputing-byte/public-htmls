Growth throughout your academic journey

The student experience within tCAP is designed to support your growth throughout your academic journey. Each step builds on the last so you continue developing career readiness, technical confidence and professional experience.

1

Stage 1 — Foundations

Students begin with onboarding, communication development, resume building, LinkedIn setup, GitHub development and goal setting.

2

Stage 2 — Power Skills

Students participate in mentorship opportunities, leadership development and networking experiences that strengthen professional confidence.

3

Stage 3 — Connectivity

Career readiness becomes more hands-on through internship preparation, employer and alumni engagement, experiential learning pathways, career fair preparation, interview coaching and applied team-based projects.

4

Stage 4 — Mastery

Students demonstrate mastery through capstones, advanced coursework, certifications and internship or career placement opportunities.
