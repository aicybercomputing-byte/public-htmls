What is tCAP?

tCAP is a structured, multiyear career development program designed exclusively for Bellini College students. Instead of waiting until their final year to focus on internships and career preparation, tCAP helps students steadily build experience, skills and professional connections throughout their academic journey.

Why tCAP matters

Employers increasingly seek graduates who can communicate effectively, collaborate on teams, solve applied problems and contribute immediately in professional environments. tCAP combines career readiness, experiential learning and employer engagement to help develop those capabilities before graduation.

tCAP pillars

Readiness · Experience · Connection

These are the three pillars that guide the tCAP experience and help students grow professionally and technically.
