Experiential Learning

Apply what you learn in real settings

tCAP experiential learning gives students a structured way to turn projects, internships, research and other approved opportunities into verified career preparation.

Process

How it works

1

Choose an experience

Internship, research, capstone, hackathon or another approved opportunity.

2

Get approval

Submit a plan of work with a faculty mentor or quality supervisor.

3

Complete the work

Build toward at least 135 verified hours and submit a midpoint update.

4

Reflect and record

Deliver a final report, pass/fail assessment and CIS 4950 notation.

Approved pathways

Ways students can build verified experience

Internships

Study abroad

Global experiences that meet program criteria.
